French Fries


The standard method for cooking french fries is deep frying,
which submerges them in hot fat, nowadays most commonly oil.
  • Potatoes
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Rosemary
  1. Slice the potatoes
  2. Soak them cold in water
  3. Rinse them twice
  4. Heat oil to 300 degrees for 5 minutes for crispy
  5. Increase heat to 400 degrees 5 minutes for golden brown
  6. Place them on paper towels with a slotted spoon
