Cheese Soup


Cheese soup is a type of soup prepared using cheese as a primary
ingredient, along with milk, broth and/or stock to form its basis.
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Flour
  • Chicken broth
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  1. Melt butter over medium-high heat
  2. Add onion and cook about 5 minutes
  3. Stir in flour to make a roux
  4. Add broth and cook stirring constantly until thickened
  5. Add milk and cook until just boiling, stirring constantly
  6. Blend the soup until smooth
  7. Cook again over medium heat until just boiling
  8. Remove from heat and whisk in chesse until melted
