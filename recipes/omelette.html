Omelette Recipe


An omelette is a dish made from eggs, fried with butter or oil in a
frying pan. It is quite common to be folded around various fillings
  • Eggs
  • Cooking oil
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  1. Season the beaten eggs with pepper and salt
  2. Heat oil and butter in a frying pan until melted
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan covering the hole surface
  4. Cook for 20 minutes then scrape a line through the middle with a spatula
  5. Fill with your favorite and scatter
  6. Fold gently in half with a spatula
  7. Slide onto a plate to serve
